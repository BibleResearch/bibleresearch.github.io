Here is my outline for chapter 2 of the book of Titus:

1. Behaviour fitting sound doctrine
    - Older men
    - Older women
    - Younger women
    - Younger men
    - Slaves
2. Motive for/cause of right living
    - God's grace
        - Brings salvation
        - Instructs us
            - To deny ungodliness
            - To live godly
            - While looking for Christ's return
    - Christ's sacrifice
        - Redeems
        - Purifies
